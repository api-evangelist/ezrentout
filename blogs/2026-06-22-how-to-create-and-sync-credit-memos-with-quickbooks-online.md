Credit adjustments are common in rental workflows. A customer may be overcharged, return equipment early, receive a refund, or need a billing correction after an invoice has already been shared. Instead of editing the original invoice, accounting teams often issue a credit memo so the customer’s credit can be tracked cleanly.

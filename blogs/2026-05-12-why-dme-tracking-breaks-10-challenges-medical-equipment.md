DME tracking doesn’t fail because of volume; it fails because of fragmented workflows. If you’re a General Manager running a medical equipment rental business, you’re not struggling because you have too many assets to manage. You’re struggling because every part of your operation, including inventory, orders, dispatch, maintenance, and billing, lives in a different place.
